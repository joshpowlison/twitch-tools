ViewerImpact

Why does this exist?

As creatives, we need to stand equal with our communities. That way we can create real connection. And that way we can create real impact.

ViewerImpact helps put us on equal footing with our viewers- they can make their voice heard on stream with us- and be a part, instead of apart.

  • Make your viewers' voices bigger. Create animations and sound effects that trigger on chat messages and channel rewards.
  • Thank people with more than the usual. Set up as many animations as you want to go off when people cheer bits, subscribe, or gift subscriptions.
  • Your friends in chat don't have to wait. You can set up within minutes and even build animations while your stream is running, on your stream.

Getting started

  1. Add browser-source.html to your streaming software. Set its width to "1920", and its height to "1080"
  2. Check Control audio via OBS (or a similarly-named option)
  3. Right-click on the item in the preview, and select Fit to Screen

Adding Files

Go into the assets folder and duplicate an existing folder. Rename it, replace its image.png and audio.mp3 files, and refresh the editor to see it.

The folder's name must only consist of A-Z and dashes, and cannot start with a dash.
